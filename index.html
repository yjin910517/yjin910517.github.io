Quantified Me

Influenced by Quantified Self, I started to quantitatively track many aspects of my life in 2016. After all, what data project can be cooler than analysis yourself? I didn't use any fancy wearable techs to automate data tracking. Some data were recorded as plain text in diary, and others were extracted from websites and apps I used. At the end of every year, I compiled an annual report about myself. Exerpt of report 2016 and 2017 can be found below. I tried to add some new attributes to quantify every year.
